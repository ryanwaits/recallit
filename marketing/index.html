Three steps, no setup.

Describe what you want to learn, practice it however you like, and the right thing comes back at the right time.

  1. 01

    Describe

    Point it at a book, a paper, a link, or just a topic in your head. It builds the cards for you and shows you the exact line each one came from.

  2. 02

    Practice

    Run a quick drill when you're busy, or talk it through when you want to explain things in your own words. You pick, and you can switch any day.

  3. 03

    Remember

    Answer well and a card drifts away for weeks. Struggle and it comes back sooner. When you sit down, what's waiting is the stuff you're about to forget.
