Why you can trust it.

The hard part was never flashcards. It's grading you honestly, and proving it.

Honest by construction

Cites a line you can check

Every card, and every point it grades you on, traces to a verbatim line from your source. What it can't verify, it holds back instead of inventing.

Code-owned grading

Graded by rule, not vibe

A deterministic rule scores your answer against the source's points. The tutor can examine and explain; it never picks the grade. No "the AI says you understood."

Yours

Your files, your call

Your cards are plain files on your disk. No accounts, no cloud, no one can change your grade, including us.

And it can talk: voice packs let you answer aloud and hear it back, on your own keys.
