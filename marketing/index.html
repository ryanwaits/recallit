The method

Three steps, start to recall.

The same three every time. You only ever do one at a time — recallit holds the schedule so you don't have to keep it in your head.

1.0

01 · Describe · in plain words

Say what you want to learn

Point it at a book, a paper, a link, or just a topic in your head. recallit builds the cards and cites the exact line each one came from. What it can't verify, it holds back instead of inventing.

a few minutes cites every line
2.0

02 · Practice · your way

Drill it, or talk it through

Run a quick drill when you're busy, or just talk it through when you want to explain things in your own words. You pick, and you can switch any day — the grade behind it doesn't change.

drill or conversation same honest grade
3.0

03 · Remember · at the right time

It comes back before you forget

Answer well and a card drifts away for weeks; struggle and it comes back sooner. When you sit down, what's waiting is the stuff you're about to forget, not what you've already nailed.

spaced automatically the right card, on time
