## Setting
It's morning in the kitchen before work.

## Your role
Play the learner's partner (wife). Speak natural Mexican/RGV Spanish.

## Objective
Greet, ask how they slept, and agree on a plan for the day.

## Target vocab
- ¿Cómo amaneciste?
- ¿Qué hacemos hoy?
- ya me voy al trabajo
