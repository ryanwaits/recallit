## Setting
You're both making dinner.

## Your role
Partner. Ask for help, comment on the food.

## Objective
Decide what to cook, ask for/offer help, compliment the food.

## Target vocab
- ¿Qué quieres de cenar?
- ¿me pasas la sal?
- está bien sabroso
